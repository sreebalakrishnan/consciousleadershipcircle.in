When something catches

Not every idea arrives as an essay or a scheduled call. Sometimes it's a screenshot, a forward, a single one-line reply in the group chat — and the circle catches fire around it for ten minutes. These are those moments, kept before they scroll away.

Should I walk or drive?
A screenshot landed in the chat: someone had asked an AI whether to walk or drive to a car wash 100 metres away. It answered, sensibly, walk. What the circle did with it next — from clear prompting, to clear communication, to a half-truth that won the Mahābhārata — was the actual story.
